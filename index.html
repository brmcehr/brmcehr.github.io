Q: How do I add a patient?
A: 1) Go to 'Patients' tab.
      2) Click 'New Patient' in the top right corner
      3) Fill in the required fields marked with a *
        i) First and last names
        ii) Date of Birth
        iii) Gender
        iV) Zip Code (can be BRMC Zip Code 21202)
      4) Fill in extra fields if you have time
      5) Click 'Add Patient' in the bottom right corner
Q: How do I create a new encounter?
A: 1) Go to 'Patients' tab.
      2) Click on the patient's name
      3) Click 'Open New Encounter"
        i) Select Encounter with: 'Dr. Group One'
        ii) Ensure it says today's date
        iii) Select the appropriate visit type
        iv) Encounter mode: In Person
        v) Chart Type: Brief
      4) Click 'Create'
      5) Fill in appropriate sections.
      6) Click 'Save' when you are done. Do not sign it off.
Q: Do we need to maintain a paper copy as well?
A: No, you DO NOT need to maintain a paper copy as well?
      You are welcome to write it on paper and copy it to an EHR,
      but we feel it's a waste of time.
Q: How do I view past encounters?
A: 1) Go to 'Patients' tab.
      2) Click on the patient's name
      3) Go to sub-tab 'Past Encounters'
      4) If you need an earlier encounter select All on the left.
